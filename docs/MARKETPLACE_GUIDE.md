## Getting Started

### For Sellers

#### 1. Create Your First Product

1. Navigate to **Marketplace ‚Üí Sell Your Product**
2. Fill in basic information:
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive product name
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation of what you're selling
   - **Category**: Select appropriate category
   - **Featured Image**: Add a product image URL

3. Set pricing:
   - **Free**: Offer for free (lead generation, portfolio)
   - **Fixed Price**: One-time purchase price
   - **Subscription**: Recurring monthly/yearly payment
   - **Custom Quote**: Price on request

4. Add details:
   - List key features (one per line)
   - Add relevant tags (comma-separated)
   - Set inventory/quantity if limited
   - Mark as limited edition if applicable

5. Review and create the listing

#### 2. Publish Your Listing

- Save as **Draft** first to review later
- Click **Publish** to make it live on the marketplace
- Published products appear in search results and recommendations

#### 3. Manage Your Sales

- **Sales Dashboard**: Overview of all metrics
- **My Products**: View and edit all your listings
- **Recent Orders**: Track incoming orders
- **Conversations**: Communicate with potential buyers

#### 4. Handle Customer Inquiries

- Buyers can ask questions directly
- Messages appear in your **Messages** section
- Respond to inquiries to increase conversion

### For Buyers

#### 1. Browse & Search

1. Go to **Marketplace ‚Üí Browse Products**
2. Use filters:
   - Search bar for keywords
   - Category filter
   - Price range filter
   - Sort options (newest, rating, price)

#### 2. View Product Details

- See full description, features, and images
- Check seller rating and reviews
- Read customer testimonials
- View pricing and availability

#### 3. Ask Questions

- Click **Ask a Question** to message seller
- Start a direct conversation
- Get answers before purchasing

#### 4. Make a Purchase

1. Click **Buy Now**
2. Review order summary
3. Enter quantity and optional message
4. Complete purchase
5. Receive order confirmation

#### 5. Leave a Review

- After purchase, leave a review
- Rate 1-5 stars
- Add title and detailed feedback
- Help other buyers make decisions

#### 6. Manage Your Orders

- **My Orders**: View purchase history
- Track order status
- Access purchased digital products
- Review seller communications

## Troubleshooting

### Can't find my product?
- Check product status (must be "Published")
- Verify it's in correct category
- Check that it matches search filters applied

### Not receiving inquiry messages?
- Check your message notifications
- Ensure seller account is active
- Verify CORS and email settings

### Payment issues?
- Verify payment method is valid
- Check for typos in payment information
- Try a different payment method
- Contact support if issue persists

### Product not showing rating?
- Need at least 1 review to show rating
- Reviews from verified purchases only
- Wait for reviews to process
